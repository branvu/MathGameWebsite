How to Play!!!

To play this game, you have to add numbers! In the beginning levels, add the previous number
and the current number and enter the sum. For example, if 2 is on the screen
and then 3 is on the screen. Add 2 and 3. Now, when another number appears-let's say 4, then you
will need to add 3 and 4 together and then if 5 appears, add 4 and 5.
You are always adding the previous number and the current number together. Look at the demo for additional help.

Initial time between numbers - 3s
Initial range of the numbers - 0-9

When you reach a high enough level, the "Increased Recall" means that instead of adding 2 numbers, you now have to add 3,
so you are adding the previous + previous + current number. This will be harder and more challenging, but the time and range will be set
back to the initial.
